ABOUT

I'm Eric Taylor

My passion for software development began well before I even considered computer programming and software development as something I could use to solve problems. I have noticed or experienced these problems on some level in my life and 20-year employment history. As an insurance agent and an owner of my own social media marketing business, I consistently found myself loving the process of solving problems. The greatest frustration I have experienced professionally in that time has been the lack of tools at my disposal to effectively solve problems that would arise in a given day. One recurring problem is the dilemma that seems to plague so many offices in the corporate world; making the choice of being in compliance with all rules and regulations or being productive. I refuse to believe these two very vital components of a successful business cannot co-exist. The solution to this, and the other various problems I would love to attempt solving can be found within the walls of the Software Development hemisphere.
